Polytechnic University of the Philippines

1016 Anonas, Sta. Mesa, Maynila, Kalakhang Maynila

List of Requirements

  1. Passed PUP College Entrance Exam Test (PUPCET)
  2. Confirmation Requirements
  3. High School Graduate
  4. High School Card (Form 138)
  5. Form 137-A
  6. Medical Clearance from PUP Medical Clinic
  7. Certification of Good Moral Character duly signed by the high school principal/guidance counselor of the school last attended.
  8. NSO authenticated copy of Birth Certificate/ Certificate of Live Birth

University Specialization

  • Education
  • Information Technology
  • Management
  • Marketing

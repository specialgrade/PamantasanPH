Freshmen Requirements

  • Long Brown Envelope containing the following:
    • Photocopy of the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) Authenticated Copy of Birth Certificate (Original copy must be presented for comparison purpose only)
    • If the entries are not readable, please secure a duly accomplished Local Civil Registrar Form 1A from the Civil Registrar’s Office where one's birth was registered.
    • If the PSA issues certification that the birth was not registered, please secure a copy of the said certification of Negative Birth Registry and the Local Birth Certificate acknowledged by the Local Civil Registrar. Photocopy of the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) Authenticated Copy of Marriage Contract, for married students only.
    • Original copy of any of the aforesaid must be presented for comparison purpose only.
    • Duly Accomplished Student Personal Record (SPR)
    • Original copy of the Duly Accomplished Department of Education (DepEd) Form 138-A (High School Card)
    • Original copy of the Certificate of Good Moral Character from School Last Attended
    • 1 pc 2x2 ID picture (recent with white background)
    • Photocopy of Parents Income Tax or Certificate from BIR
    • Medical Certificate with Chest X-ray result (not yet required due to MECQ but must be accomplished once the MECQ is lifted)
    • Freshmen Checklist and Authorization which is downloadable at Registrar FB Page: https://www.facebook.com/CSUOAS

Application Process

For Freshmen:

  1. Register online at https://myschool.carsu.edu.ph/index.php by filling out the application for admission form.
  2. Click save and take note of the Application Number.
  3. Double-check the information you have entered. OAS will not make changes to the information you have provided, which will appear on your Student Personal Record.

For Transferees and Returnees:

  1. Secure a Slots Availability Form at the Office of Admission and Scholarship.
  2. Proceed to the Department and bring your Transcript of Records for evaluation.
  3. Register online at https://myschool.carsu.edu.ph/index.php by filling out the application for admission form. Click save and take note of the Application Number.
  4. Set an appointment at https://admission.carsu.edu.ph/sites/online_appointment/
  5. Click SIGN IN. Use your application number as your username and your date of birth (YYYY-MM-DD) as the password to Sign In at the Online Appointment page. (Example: Username: 2023-00001 | Password: 2009-01-29)
  6. Show up on your Appointment date (Examination Date).
  7. Show up on your appointment date and bring the following requirements:
  • 1 pc. Passport Size ID Picture with white background (dress code: formal/proper) (No Picture, NO EXAM)
  • Valid ID, any of the following: (School ID, Voter's ID, Voter's Certification, Driver's License, Postal ID, Passport, Police Clearance, or any Government issued ID; (NO ID, NO EXAM))
  • Application Number
  • Pencil (number 2, any brand)
  • Ballpen
  • Face mask
  • Alcohol/hand sanitizer.

Your room assignment will be posted a day before your confirmed appointment. For your confirmed room assignment, please visit the Office of Admission and Scholarship Facebook page at: https://www.facebook.com/CSUOAS/

Reminder:

  • NO COMPLETE REQUIREMENTS, NO EXAM.
  • WALK-IN APPLICANTS ARE NOT ALLOWED. ONLY SCHEDULED APPLICANTS MAY TAKE THE ADMISSION TEST.
  • APPLICANTS CAN ONLY TAKE THE TEST ONCE.

In line with the safety protocols declared by the DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH (DOH), IF YOU HAVE FEVER, COUGH AND/OR COLDS PLEASE DO NOT SHOW UP ON YOUR EXAMINATION DATE AND CALL OUR OFFICE to inform about your situation.

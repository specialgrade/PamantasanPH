Admission Requirements

  • Old Students: Passbook
  • New Students:
    1. One (1) Brown Envelope long
    2. FORM 138-A (High School Report Card)
    3. Certificate of Good Moral Character
    4. Birth Certificate (NSO)
    5. Three (3) pieces 2x2 picture
    6. College Entrance Test Result
    7. Aptitude Test Result (BS Crim, BSED, BEED, BSN, and BSCS)
    8. Police Clearance from Police Station of the Enrollee's origin (MALE) For Freshman Students. OSAS Requirements.
  • Transferees - Additional Requirements:
    1. Transcript of Records (TOR) Original Copy for Evaluation
    2. Honorable Dismissal (H.D.) Original Copy

Application Process

  1. Submit your enrollment requirements at the Office of the Admission.
  2. Proceed to Registrar's office, OSAS, Guidance Office for Clearance.
  3. Fill up the Registration Form and Enrollment Form then proceed to your respective colleges for listing of courses (subjects) to be taken.
  4. Proceed to the Assessment Office, present your Certificate of Registration (COR) for assessment of fees.
  5. Proceed to the Collecting Office (Cashier), present your Assessment Form for payment of fees.

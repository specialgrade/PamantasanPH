Bohol Island State University - Clarin

Poblacion Norte, Clarin, Philippines

NOT YET OPENED

Admission Requirements

  • A Certification that the applicant is currently enrolled in Senior High School duly signed by the Principal or by an authorized personnel
  • Certified true copy of Form 137 in Grade 10 and Grade 11
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 2x2 Picture, formal, recent, with a nametag and plain background
  • Informative copy of Transcript of Records (for Transferees)
    • In case a student has been enrolled in other HEI and wants to transfer to BISU but has dropped or withdrawn all subjects, a certified true copy of Grade 11 and 12 will be evaluated. They can only apply in the Non-Board courses.
  • Certificate of General Weighted Average (for Transferees)
  • Note: The grades in Grade 10 and Grade 11 are evaluated against the grade requirement for the curricular program applied for by the applicants for Admission purposes ONLY.
  • Additional Data (IF APPLICABLE)
    • Indigenous People – Certificate of Tribe Membership, or any equivalent certification, as belonging to indigenous people/indigenous cultural community
    • PWD – Disability Certificate from DSWD or PWD Identification Card

Application Process

  1. Click on the Apply Now button.
  2. Fill in the application form completely.
  3. Upload clear scanned copies of all the required documents.
  4. Review that all the information you provided are correct, and all required documents uploaded are complete.
  5. Click on the Submit button to complete your application.

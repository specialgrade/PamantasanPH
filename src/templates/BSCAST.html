Application Process

  1. Entrance Requirement. All new students must apply for admission at the Guidance. Testing and Admission Office for Secondary, under graduate programs and graduate programs. They must submit the prescribed application credentials and must qualify in the Admission Test.
  2. Laboratory High School. Only applicants with an academic average of at least 83% and above and no grades below 80% in any subject in grade school can take the high school admission test.
  3. College of Engineering and Architecture. Only applicants with a high school academic average of at least 83% and above, no grades below 85% in Mathematics, English, and Science/Physics and no grades below 80% in any subject can take the College Admission Test.
  4. College of Education. Only applicants with a high school academic average of 83% and above and no grades below 85% in English, Mathematics, and Science/Physics for Malh, English and Physical Science Majors; and no grades below 80% in other subjects can take the College Admission Test.
  5. College of Trades and Technology. Only high school graduates can take the College Admission Test.
  6. College of Arts and Sciences. Only applicants with no failing grades in high school can take the College Admission Test.

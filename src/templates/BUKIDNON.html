Bukidnon, Malaybay City, Philippines

NOT YET OPENED

Admission Requirements

  1. Personal E-mail Address
  2. For currently enrolled Grade 12 students: Certification from the office of the school principal confirming applicant as Grade 12 student or Grade 12 Report Card
  3. For Senior High School graduates: Report Card or High School Diploma
  4. For Transferees: Transcript of Records/Honorable Dismissal or Certification from the Office of the Registrar confirming applicant's current enrollment to the institution.
  5. For Returnees (enrolled in college, but stopped studying in BukSU before 2018): Certification from the Registrar to be requested via this form (https://forms.gle/aKoX7zTJmnTSBNNFA)
  6. Soft copy of 2”x2” ID picture. (applicant must be wearing white t-shirt on white background)
  7. Accurately filled out the necessary information on the BukSU-CAT website.

Application Process

  1. NEW STUDENT ENROLLMENT GUIDE
  2. Step 1
    • Log-in at admissions.buksu.edu.ph
  3. Step 2
    • In your dashboard, click your admissions status
    • In the Admission Overview, click show exam result.
    • Then click proceed to enrollment.
  4. NOTE: Please contact the Admission and Testing Office if there are no test results.
  5. Apply for Enrollment (6 steps in this process)
    • Campus Selection
    • Course Selection
    • Course Requirements
    • Learning Modality Preference
    • Academic Information
    • Enrollment Confirmation
      • NOTE: In the enrollment confirmation, you need to confirm and sign your application, unsigned applications won’t be able to complete the enrollment process.
  6. After the confirmation of enrollment, please wait for the evaluator to evaluate you and after the approval of your application, you may download your Certificate of Registration (COR), if it’s incorrect, please click the Report Incorrect COR.

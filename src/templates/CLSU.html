    • Application Process

    1. ENROLLMENT PROCEDURE FOR INCOMING FIRST YEAR
    2. https://ctec.clsu2.edu.ph/clsucat/REPORT on your enrollment schedule.
    3. Kindly report to the Office of Admissions (OAd) on the schedule assigned to your program. Please note that failure to attend on the scheduled date without prior notice will result in your slot being forfeited. For rescheduling, please email your request to new_resched@clsu2.edu.ph.
    4. (Follow our Facebook page at https://www.facebook.com/CLSUofficeofadmissions for updates and announcement)
    5. PRESENT your entrance credentials.
    6. Grade 12 SF 9 or Form 138 (Original copy)*
    7. OAD Enrollment Form (parent/guardian signed)
    8. OSA Individual Record Form (applicant signed)
    9. PSA/NSO Birth Certificate (Original copy)*
    10. 2"x2" color photo (3 pcs. recent with white background and name tag with the following format: SURNAME, FIRST NAME, MIDDLE INITIAL.)
    11. Grade 12 SF 10 or Form 137 (Original copy) (Request letters will be given during enrollment.)
    12. *Note: If you are unable to provide any required credentials, a promissory note must be presented. A template for this promissory note is included in our email regarding the enrollment procedure.
    13. Here's the step on how to update and download your OAD Enrollment Form and OSA Individual Form
    14. Log in to your CTEC portal with this link: https://ctec.clsu2.edu.ph/clsucat/"
    15. Below the admission letter you can see 4 tabs then Click the Download
    16. Print the following:
    17. Enrollment Form - A4 paper, colored
    18. OSA Form - 8.5 "*13" Colored, Back to back
    19. Medical and Dental Form - A4, Colored
    20. Download, print, then sign the Individual Record Form and Enrollment Form by your parent (EITHER your father or your mother) or guardian. An SF 10/Form 137 request letter will be provided by Office of Admissions during enrollment in case Form 137 is not available.
    21. Admission Slip will be given to Applicants with approved credentials:
    22. Apply for a lodging permit (c/o Student Housing Unit at CLIRDEC)
    23. Report for your health/medical record (The medical and dental forms are available for download in your CTEC portal.) (c/o CLSU Infirmary at CLIRDEC).
    24. Submit your completed admission slip (c/o CLSU OAD).
    25. Deadline for submission of promised Entrance Credential and Withdrawal of Enrollment is on July 22, 2024. Note: Non-submission of all documents will result to "unofficially enrolled" status.

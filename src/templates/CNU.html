Cebu Normal University

San Antonio, Osmeña Boulevard, Cebu City, Philippines

Admission Requirements

  • Certified true copy of Senior High School Report Card, showing a GPA of 85, Learners Reference Number (LRN), and Senior High Track and Strand (Grade 12 – 1st quarter or 2nd quarter grading) (scanned or picture copy); For Transferee, Certified True Copy of the Transcript of Records
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character (scanned or picture copy)
  • PSA issued Birth Certificate (scanned or picture copy)
  • Scanned or picture copy of a valid Identification Card (front and back images)
  • Accomplished College Admission Form (Profile Sheet and Testing Form)
  • 2×2 colored picture with name and signature (scanned or picture copy)
  • One active email address to prevent missed emails/information/instructions

Application Process

  1. APPLICANT fills out the google form for College / Department / Campus applied
  2. COLLEGE/DEPARTMENT/CAMPUS evaluates the applicant’s requirements
  3. (APPLICANTS WHO PASSED THE EVALUATION) The College / Department / Campus authorized representative forwards the application to the Testing Center for verification of residency
  4. The TESTING CENTER verifies the residency of the applicant. If the applicant currently lives in the Philippines, he/she will receive instructions through email from the Testing Center to fill out the TESTING FORM 2
  5. The APPLICANT prepares the following documents for uploading on the Testing Form 2:
    • College Admission Form (Signed by the Dean, download from the verification email from the Testing Center)
    • Valid Identification Card with picture & signature
    • One (1) decent selfie close-up picture
  6. The APPLICANT will accomplish the Undergraduate Testing Form 2 and attach the necessary documents mentioned above (Must be accomplished within 10 days after receiving the email from the Testing Center)
  7. The TESTING STAFF emails the Testing Schedule & Testing Guidelines 3-5 working days after the applicant filled out the TESTING FORM 2. If Testing Schedules are not yet finalized, the applicant will still receive email from the Testing Center confirming that his/her response on the Testing Form 2 was received and Testing Schedule is to follow.
  8. The APPLICANT takes the face to face admission test on the given schedule and waits for the releasing of Qualifiers for Interview. If Qualified, the APPLICANT appears on the interview schedule and follows the instructions of the interviewer.

Application Process

  1. REGISTRATION PROCESS
    • STEP 1: Verification of Credentials and Approval of Subject/Course Load - Present the interview slip from the guidance office together with the required credentials for registration to the Program Head of your chosen program for verification. Program Head will Provide the Registration Form and List of Subjects/courses to be enrolled. Completely fill out form and have it approved by the Institute Dean.
    • STEP 2: Health Assessment - Proceed to the College Clinic for Health Assessment
    • STEP 3: Registration of Subjects/Courses to be Enrolled - Present duly accomplished Registration Form together with the required registration requirements to the Registrar's Office for registration of subjects/courses to be enrolled. Wait for the issuance of Student Actual Subject Load.
    • STEP 4: Validation of Student's Account - Free Higher Education (FHE) Grantees shall proceed to the Assessment Office for Validation of Student's Account. Non FHE shall Proceed to the Cashier's Office for the partial payment of School Fees. Note: Processing of School ID is at the Production Office.
  2. FOR OLD/CONTINUING STUDENTS
    • STEP 1: Evaluation of Academic Records - Secure grades at the STUDENT KIOSK. Present duly signed CLEARANCE and Grades to the Program Head for Evaluation.
    • STEP 2: Approval of Subject/Course Load - Program Head will provide the Registration Form and List of Subjects/courses to be enrolled. Completely fill out form and have it approved by the Institute Dean.
    • STEP 3: Registration of Subjects/Courses to be Enrolled - Present duly accomplished Registration Form together with the duly signed clearance to the Registrar's Office for registration of subjects/courses to be enrolled. Wait for the issuance of Student Actual Subject Load.
    • STEP 4: Validation of Student's Account - Free Higher Education (FHE) Grantees shall proceed to the Assessment Office for Validation of Student's Account. Non FHE shall Proceed to the Cashier's Office for the partial payment of School Fees.

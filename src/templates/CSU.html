      Application Process

      1. For the Main campus, applicant will https://apps.cusu.edu.ph/admission/ access this link and fill-out the online application. In filling out the online form, remember the following
        • Do not edit (tamper) the information-data of your application form outside the online admission system.
        • The declared strand in the application form and the strand should match
        • Choose the correct entry type of applicant, do not be confused with the category of applicant.

          • Thoroughly check all information inputted in the online admission system before saving and submitting to avoid typographical errors, wrong spelling, incomplete, or wrong entry of information.

          Once there is a control number, the information details cannot be edited again.

      2. Applicant will download the accomplished online application form and attach a IxI ID picture and affix his/her/their signature.
        • If a soft copy of the ID picture will be used, ensure that it will not affect the format of the application form.
        • Do not forget to affix signature in the application form
      3. Applicants will select from the admission system their preferred date and time of submission of their application and save the schedule. It is important to save the schedule
      4. On the saved date, applicant will submit their application together with the complete required documents at the Student Union Building (SUB), second floor, lobby area.
        • Only applicants who are scheduled on the particular date will be accommodated. No-walk ins for submission.
      5. Once the application has been collected by the Office, applicants will receive their Exam Permit. The schedule (date and time) of admission examination is indicated in the permit.
      6. All transferee-applicants will only undergo college evaluation and will not take the admission exam. Result of evaluation from the college shall determine if the applicant is qualified to transfer or not.
      7. TCP applicants will only undergo college evaluation from College of Education (CED) and will not take the admission exаm.

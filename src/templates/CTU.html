Admission Requirements

    Senior High School Graduates/ Graduating Students

    • Senior High School Report Card (Grade 12) as of 1st Semester AY 2020-2021 signed by the School Principal for Senior High School graduating students

    • Grade 11 Report Card for Math, Science and English (Board Programs only)
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character
    • Close-Up selfie picture with a name tag (JPEG format)

    Alternative Learning System (ALS) Graduates

    • ALS Examination Rating
    • Certificate of Eligibility to College

    • Close-Up selfie picture with a name tag (JPEG format)

Application Process

The Admission Office caters to student-applicants who are interested in applying in Cebu Technological University (CTU) Cebu City Mountain (CCM) only. The application must be made online through the admissions portal at ordir.ctu.edu.ph/admission/application-form. All queries about admissions will be catered through the official FB Pages, e-mail, and contact number.

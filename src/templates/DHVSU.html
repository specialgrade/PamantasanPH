Admission Requirements

  1. ONLINE ADMISSION APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
  2. Student applicants must upload the following documents to the online admission portal during their application for the desired program. Applicants with incomplete/invalid submitted requirements will not be processed.
  3. A. College First Year
  4. Certified True Copy of Senior High School (SHS) Form 137/Form 138 from Grade 11 to 2nd quarter of Grade 12;
  5. Certificate of Grade weighted average:
  6. Parents/Guardians' latest Income Tax Return (ITR) or Certification of Tax Exemption (for Low Income Family); and
  7. Certificate of Good Moral Character;
  8. Original PSA/NSO Birth Certificate or Marriage Certificate for female married applicant;
  9. Two (2) pcs 2x2 size color picture, white background with nametag:
  10. Completely accomplished College profiling via online admission portal.
  11. Additional requirements for BSN qualifiers:
  12. Psychometric assessment
  13. Physical fitness certified by government physician with laboratory results (Chest x-ray, CBC, Urinalysis)

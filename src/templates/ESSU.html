Eastern Samar State University

Brgy. Maypangdan, Borongan, Philippines

Requirements

  • Accomplished Entrance Examination Application Form
  • 1 Photocopy of Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 2 pcs. Passport-size ID picture in white background

Admission Requirements

For Incoming First Year:

  • 1 photocopy of form 138

For Transfer Student:

  • 1 photocopy of transcript of records or information copy of grades
  • Honorable dismissal

Application Process

Applicants shall proceed to the Admissions Office on the 1st Floor of the ICTC Building (behind the Central Library), ESSU-Borongan to secure an application form and submit their requirements.

Dates of Examination in ESSU-Borongan:

  • May 17, 24, 31, 2024
  • June 10, 11, 13, 14, 17-21, 2024

Entrance Examination will also be conducted at ESSU-Balangiga Extension Campus on May 10, 2024 and ESSU-Arteche Extension Campus on April 26, 2024.

Application Process

  1. Log in to our website at https://apps.evsu.edu.ph/admissions/confirmation_login using your Application Number and Birthdate or your LRN as your password.
  2. After logging in successfully, carefully read and comprehend all of the information included in your admission results. Then, click the "CONFIRM INTENT TO ENROLL" button to proceed to the next steps.

Note: FOR APPLICANTS ON THE WAITING LIST STATUS, you must apply for a position on the waitlist, which must be authorized by the Head of Department. Your application will be accepted if a slot becomes available.

Admission Requirements

  • Certificate of Grades (1st Semester AY 2020-2021)
  • Statement of Account (SOA)
  • Photocopy of your CEDULA 2021
  • Liquidation for 2nd Semester AY 2019-2020
  • Photocopy of your School ID

  • UNDERGRADUATE COURSES/PROGRAMS

    • Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA) - Major in Financial Management
    • Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEEd)
    • Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd) - Major in English, Mathematics, Values Education

Application Process

  1. For New/Transferees
    • Take the entrance examination at the Guidance Counselor’s Office.
    • Submit Medical Certificate at the School Clinic.
    • Submit the required documents and ask for Pre-Registration Form at the Registrar’s Office.
    • Proceed for evaluation at the respective Department Adviser’s Office.
    • Proceed for enlistment at the Registrar’s Office.
    • Proceed for confirmation at the Finance Office.
    • Claim your Certificate of Registration at the Registrar’s Office.
  2. For Old/Returnees
    • If you are a graduating student, accomplish the clearance from the Finance office.
    • Submit Medical Certificate at the School Clinic.
    • Submit the lacking documents and ask for Pre-Registration Form at the Registrar’s Office.
    • Proceed for evaluation at the respective Department Adviser’s Office.
    • Proceed for enlistment at the Registrar’s Office.
    • Proceed for confirmation at the Finance Office.
    • Claim your Certificate of Registration at the Registrar’s Office.

Failure to come within the arranged appointment will be re-scheduled and you will be notified. Please be reminded to strictly follow the mandated health protocols while in the vicinity of the school and bring the hard copy of the initial form you filled out.

    Application Process

    1. Mode of Enrolment: In-person/Face-to-Face
  • 1. Medical
  • - for Procedural Health Check-up (thermal scan, health declaration, alcohol wash, face shield, etc.) and other health concerns.
  • 2. Registrar and Guidance
  • - clients will submit entrance/admission credentials or documents and acquire enrolment forms and guidance inventory form and other pertinent forms needed for registration. (route sheet, temporary enrolment form, registration form, etc.)
  • 3. College Department (CAFED, CBME, CTE, CCS, Graduate School)
  • - students shall present themselves to their respective departments for evaluation and subject loading and giving of class schedules.
  • - after getting their approve class schedules and subjects, they are required to fill in the needed information in the Registration Card, more particularly in front and at the back. Please let your Program Deans affixed his/her signature on the Registration Card.
  • 4. Scholarship
  • - if the student has some concern about Scholarship, please proceed to the Scholarship Table/Booth.
  • 5. Encoding (Registrar) students shall present their approve and duly filled-up Registration Card for proper encoding and enlistment in the roster of enrolled students.
  • 6. Accounting/Assessment
  • - after the encoding of the approved subject loads, the student proceeds to the Accounting/Assessment section for assessment of fees and printing of Certificate of Enrollment
  • Note: Miscellaneous and Tuition Fees of qualified CHED UniFAST grantees is FREE
  • 7. NSTP/ROTC
  • - new and old students, transferees (inbound) who have not yet finished or graduated NSTP or ROTC shall proceed to the NSTP/ROTC Table/Booth for evaluation and registration.
  • 8. Releasing (Registrar)
  • - classcards and student copies of the registration documents due to the students will be released in this section.

    Application Process

    1. APPLICATION PROCEDURES
    2. Go to https://isu-enrollment.online/ online application and provide the following details:
  • Campus you want to enroll
  • Pre-entry Status
  • Level of Education to apply
  • Complete the online application form by keying the following basic informations:
  • Personal Information
  • Educational Background
  • Contact Information
  • Choose your prefered course
  • Note: Admission of students will be based on their General Weighted Average (GWA) in the SHS Card. For programs with licensure examinations, a GWA with at least 87% will be required.
  • Submit the required documents.
  • Note: In line with the general community quarantine implemented by the government, we suggest that you submit the initial requirements for evaluation and ranking. ID picture must have a plain background for clearer identification.
  • All original application documents must be submitted on a later date to be announced by the Office of the Registrar.
  • Data Privacy Notice By accomplishing the online application and submitting to the Office of Student Affairs Services, the applicant adheres to the commitment of Isabela State University (ISU) to protect the privacy of the personal information of applicants as required by the Data Privacy Act of 2012.
  • Submission of incomplete requirements will not be processed.
  • Falsification, misrepresentation, or withholding of information will automatically nullify application and enrollment in ISU.
  • CONFIRMATION OF YOUR APPLICATION
  • As soon as you agree with the data privacy notice of the online admission, you will see a notification of successfully submitted your application for admission. You will be notified of your Reference # and a notification will be sent to your email.
  • Note: please check your email regularly and please remember or take a photo of your reference number for future use.

Application Process

  1. Online Portal Pre-Ranking Guide to the Applicants

    STEP 1. Enter the information requested in order to log in. Specify the campus where you took the test, including your application number, then your first and last name. To log in, click the button.

    STEP 2. Click the "Select Course" button after logging in.

    STEP 3. Kindly provide the appropriate data, particularly the SHS General Average. Enter your first semester grade if you are a SHS graduating student.

    STEP 4. Select 2 (two) courses based on your admission exam result.

    STEP 5. This is the last part; but if you entered incorrect information by mistake, particularly about your grades, remove every course selected before choosing a new one.

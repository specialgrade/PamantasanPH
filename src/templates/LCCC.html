La Carlota City College

Gurrea Street, La Carlota City, Negros Occidental Philippines

CLOSED

Admission Requirements

  1. High School Graduate

    • Report Card (Form 138-A)
    • Two ID Pictures 2x2
    • Birth Certificate (Photocopy)NSO
    • Certificate of good Moral Character (Original)
    • Police Clearance (for Criminology students)
    • Laboratory & X-ray (for CHA & DM students)

  2. Transferee

    • Transfer Credential or Honorable Dismissal from the school last attended
    • Transcript of Records
    • Two ID pictures 2x2
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character from the school last attended
    • Birth Certificate (Photocopy) NSO

  3. Cross Enrollee

    • Two ID pictures 2x2
    • Permit to enroll from the School Registrar

  4. Returning Students

    • Two ID pictures 2x2
    • School clearance from the office of the Administrative Officer

Application Process

  1. Go to LCCC Automate Online Admission
  2. Click “Proceed”
  3. Fill up all the required details. Click “I Agree” then Submit.
  4. Take note of your reference number. If you have corrections to your data, click “Edit again?”
  5. For the schedule of exams, please wait for your name to be posted on the La Carlota City College Guidance Services Facebook page and La Carlota City College Official Facebook page.

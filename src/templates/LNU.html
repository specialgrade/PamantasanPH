Leyte Normal University

P. Paterno St., Brgy. 50., Tacloban City, Leyte, Philippines

NOT YET OPENED
Admission Requirements
  • Authenticated copy of Grade 12 Report Card with first semester grades
  • LRN and Strand
  • Transcript of Records of last school attended (for transferees only)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • PSA live birth photocopy
  • One passport-size ID photo with a plain white background
  • Long Brown Envelope

Application Process

  1. Pre-Online Enrollment
  2. Submission of Application Form and Evaluation of Admission Documents (February 15-March 27, 2024, excluding Sundays and holidays)
  3. Administration of the LNU-CAT (March 4-27, 2024, except on weekends and holidays)
  4. On-site interview (March 16, April 13 & 27, and May 11 & 25, 2024)
  5. Releasing of Result (May 27-31, 2024)

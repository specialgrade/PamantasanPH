    • Application Process

    1. MMSU
    2. HOW TO ACCESS
    3. Open a browser then go to
    4. https://mmsu.edu.ph
    5. Scroll to Quick Links then
    6. click Student Portal
    7. Online Freshman
    8. Admission System-Users
    9. Guide
    10. LOGIN
    11. Login to the system
    12. using your
    13. MCAT account
    14. REQUIREMENTS
    15. Meet the following
    16. requirements
    17. Click to Upload
    18. photo
    19. Click to browse a photo
    20. from your device
    21. Click Upload
    22. Answer the survey
    23. Click the Personal Data Sheet
    24. Fill out all required
    25. fields then click Update
    26. Click the Birth
    27. Certificate
    28. Click to select image from your device
    29. Click to Upload
    30. Click Form 138 and other
    31. Click to select image from your device
    32. Click to Upload
    33. Click CGMRC
    34. Click to select image from your device
    35. Click to Upload
    36. Click Health History
    37. Answer all the question then click Save
    38. APPLICATION
    39. If you meet all the requirements,you can now select your degree and submit application.
    40. Once the application has been submitted, wait for the approval of the Department
    41. APPROVED
    42. Once the application has been approved,the system will provide you your student number and password that you will use during the enlistment.
    43. YOU ARE NOW OFFICIALLY ADMITTED
    44. HOWEVER YOU NEED TO TAKE THE MEDICAL
    45. EXAM TO PROCEED TO ENLISTMENT
    46. APPROVED
    47. Click the Medical and Dental
    48. Download the Laboratory
    49. Request Form
    50. Present the laboratory result
    51. to your physician during
    52. medical/dental examination
    53. DONE
    54. YOU ARE NOW READY FOR ENLISTMENT
    55. JUST WAIT FOR FURTHER ANNOUNCEMENTS

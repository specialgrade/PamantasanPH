Northwestern Mindanao State College of Science and Technology

Brgy. Labuyo, Tangub City, Misamis Occidental, Philippines

NOT YET OPENED

Admission Requirements

  • Admission to the college shall be made without regard to race, color, age, national origin, gender, marital status and belief.
  • THE COLLEGE WELCOMES STUDENTS SEEKING ADMISSION WHO ARE:
    • Senior student of DepEd accredited schools;
    • Graduate of DepEd - accredited schools or secondary schools abroad;
    • Holders of Philippine Educational Placement Test (PEPT)/Accreditation and Equivalency Test (AET) Certificate or Alternative Learning System (ALS) Certificate issued by the Department of Education (DepEd);
    • Transferees

Requirements for Enrollment

  • FOR JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL
    • Entrance Test Result and admission slip issued by the principal.
    • Original copy of Form 138 and Philippine Educational Placement Test PEPT Certification starting that the student is qualified for the level
    • Certificate of good moral character certified by the principal or guidance counselor
    • Certificate of Honors (for top-five (5) honor students only)
    • PSA copy of Birth Certificate
    • 2x2 picture (2 pcs.)
    • Long brown envelope
  • FOR SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL
    • Entrance Test Result and admission slip issued by the principal.
    • Original copy of Form 138 and Philippine Educational Placement Test PEPT Certification stating that the student is qualified for the level
    • Certificate of good moral character certified by the principal or guidance counselor
    • PSA copy of Birth Certificate
    • Certificate of Completion (Grade 10)
    • ESC Certification (From Private Schools)
    • 2x2 picture (2 pcs.)
  • UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS
    • FOR FRESHMEN (HIGH SCHOOL GRADUATES WHO HAVE NOT ATTENDED COLLEGE)
      • Entrance Test Result issued by the Career Development and Counselling Office
      • Original High School Report Card or Form 138/PEPT/ALS Certificate
      • Good Moral
      • PSA Birth Certificate (Photocopy and Original)
      • Two (2) copies 2x2 pictures with name tag (with white background)
      • Police Clearance
      • One (1) copy of 1x1 picture
      • Long Brown Envelope
    • FOR TRANSFEREES. (STUDENTS WHO HAVE ALREADY TAKEN SOME COLLEGE PROGRAMS OR HAVE ALREADY ENROLLED IN OTHER HEIS)
      • Entrance Test Result issued by the Career Development & Counselling Office
      • Transfer credentials (Certificate of Honorable Dismissal)
      • Good Moral
      • Original and authenticated copy of Transcript of Records / Informative copy of grades
      • PSA Birth Certificate (Photocopy and Original)
      • Two (2) copies 2x2 pictures with name tag (with white background)
      • Police Clearance
      • Long Brown Envelope
    • FOR CONTINUING STUDENTS (COMMONLY KNOWN AS OLD STUDENTS)
      • Student Clearance
      • Accomplished Temporary Registration Form
    • FOR RE-ADMISSION (STUDENTS WHO WOULD LIKE TO CONTINUE THEIR PROGRAM AFTER TAKING LEAVE OF ABSENCE)
      • Copy of Approved Leave of Absence (ALA)
      • Latest 2x2 ID pictures with white background

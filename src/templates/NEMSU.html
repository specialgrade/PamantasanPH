Application Process

Pre-register at https://pre-enrollment.sdssu.edu.ph

For new students, click "sign-up" and choose your student status to create a new SDSSU Account.

Read the instructions and fill up all the necessary information needed.

Upload a soft copy (PDF or image file) of:

  • SHS Report Card
  • Honorable Dismissal Transfer Credentials (Transferee)
  • Transcript of Record (Transferee)
  • PSA Birth Certificate

Wait for an SMS notification after the screening of your application by the Program/Coordinators and/or Dean.

After the confirmation, you can log in back to add your subjects. You can wait for your official Certificate of Registration to be uploaded in your SDSSU Account.

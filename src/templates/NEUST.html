Admission Requirements

  1. GENERAL REQUIREMENTS FOR ADMISSION: FOR FRESHMEN:
  2. Senior high school graduate who has not taken and is not taking any college degree in other higher educational institution;
  3. Student applicant has a General Weighted Average (GWA) of 85% for Board Programs;
  4. Applicant with GWA lower than 85% may still be admitted but only for non-board programs;
  5. Must pass the NEUST-College Admission Test (NEUST-CAT);
  6. Accomplished application form, photocopy of Form 138 (SHS card), two copies of 2×2 pictures with name tag in white background; and Certificate of Good Moral Character.
  7. Some course has a minimum height requirements
  8. BS Nursing (5’0)
  9. BS Criminology(5’2)
  10. BS Hospitality Management (5’0)

    • Application Process

    1. PROCEDURE
    2. ONLINE ADMISSION PROCEDURES
    3. Log in to Applicant's Profile Page
    4. (www.admission.neust.edu.ph)using the username and password given by the Office of Admission and Registration of NEUST.
    5. Upon login to admission page using the valid username and password, applicant must upload/submit soft copy of the following:
    6. 2x2 Photograph
    7. Selfie picture
    8. Electronic signature using blue ink with white background
    9. When requirements are uploaded already, click request email confirmation. Applicant must check his/her email to get the verification code.
    10. Examination code/key and the schedule of online examination will be sent to the applicant. The examination code/key will serve as the applicant's access code to take the ONLINE EXAM and can only be used once. Applicant must ensure that he/she has a stable internet connection before taking the admission test.
    11. After taking the admission test, the applicant will wait for the result of his/her test. The Office of Admission and Registration will post the list of qualified applicants in the University's website (www.neust.edu.ph) aside from the message that he/she will receive in her/his email. If qualified, then the applicant may proceed to the ONLINE ENROLMENT.

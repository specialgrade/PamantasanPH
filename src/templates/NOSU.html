Admission Requirements

    For Incoming Freshmen / First Year Students

    • NORSU Admission Test (NAT)
    • English Qualifying Exam
    • Form 138 or High School Report Card
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character signed by the High School Principal
    • 2 copies of NSO Birth Certificate
    • Four 2" x 2" colored pictures with white background with name printed at the back
    • Mailing stamps worth Php 20 inserted in a long ordinary envelope
    • Three long-size brown envelopes to contain all the above

    For Transferes

    • NORSU Admission Test (NAT)
    • English Qualifying Exam
    • Official Transcript of Records and Honorable Dismissal
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character signed by the High School Principal
    • 2 copies of NSO Birth Certificate
    • Four 2" x 2" colored pictures with white background with name printed at the back
    • Mailing stamps worth Php 20 inserted in a long ordinary envelope
    • Three long-size brown envelopes to contain all the above

    Admission Requirements for Foreign Nationals

    • Copy of unexpired Alien Certificate of Recognition (ACR) I-Card from the Bureau of Immigration
    • Copy of unexpired Student VISA
    • Letter of intent to study (indicating the course) at NORSU addressed to the University Registrar (email: norsuregistrar@yahoo.com)
    • Photocopies of :
      • Valid Passport where the point of entry stamp is indicated
      • Valid Passport where the personal information is indicated
    • Red Ribbon or Apostille Certificate (to be secured from the Philippine Department of Foreign Affairs) of the following:
      • Affidavit of Support (if education is supported by parents, guardians)
      • Transcript of Records for College Level or Secondary Certificate Completion (if applicable)
      • Birth Certificate
      • No Derogatory Record (NBI Clearance if already in the Philippines)
    • Personal History Statement (refer to Bureau of Immigration Form)
    • 2 x 2 colored pictures with white background taken within 1 month (2 copies)
    • Location Map of Dumaguete City address, local contact number, local person of reference

Application Process

    First Year Students and Transferees

  1. Applicants will be advised on the program they shall enroll taking into consideration their First, Second and Third Priority Program.
  2. Lifelong Learners shall be enrolled in BLOCK SECTIONS to get the maximum number of enrollees based on the Enrollment Capacity of the program.
  3. Lifelong Learners shall undergo a two-semester post admission BRIDGING Program. * The Bridging and Enhancement Programs are embedded in the curriculum.
  4. ALS passers Certification must bear the notation that they are "ELIGIBLE TO ENROLL IN COLLEGE"

    5. Off-Track Enhancement Program

  5. OFF-TRACK SHS Graduates shall enroll in at most four (4) credit-bearing subjects taken from the SHS specialization subjects or other requisite subjects (distributed in two semesters-part of the Off-Track Enhancement Program) aligned to the course that they are applying for.
  6. The subjects under the Off-Track Enhancement Program shall be enrolled as overload subjects (on top of the regular loads of the students).

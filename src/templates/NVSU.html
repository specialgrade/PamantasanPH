    Application Process

    1. OPEN YOUR BROWSER. (Google Chrome, Mozila, etc.) go to https://sias.nvsu.edu.ph/then
    2. SELECT YOUR PREFERRED CAMPUS
    3. Click the ADMISSION.
    4. Click the New Application check box to get your Control No.
    5. Make sure to Fill out the Personal Data Section accurately.
    6. Note: Kindly disregard "Member of Cultural Minority" and "Dual Citizenship".
    7. Fill out the Admission Data Section correctly.
    8. Click the Dropdown Menu and choose College
    9. Review the details in your application before saving.
    10. You will be notified once your application is approved or you can visit:
    11. https://sias.nvsu.edu.ph/ to check the status of your application

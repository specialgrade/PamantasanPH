Application Process

Admission for AY 2024-2025

The admission of maritime programs for the AY 2024-2025 first semester is from Jan. 22, 2024 to Mar. 31, 2024, the other programs are from Mar. 4, 2024 to May 2024. The requirements will be displayed at the Admission System.

For admission concerns, contact the PIT-Guidance Office, you may send a message in their Facebook page, call/message (053)555-9841 / 09198676255, or email online-admission@pit.edu.ph. Don't forget to like and follow the PIT-Guidance Office page for proper guidance and future announcements regarding admissions.

ON-GOING

Admission Requirements

  1. Certified True Copy Report Card with no failing grade in any subject
  2. Two copies of 2x2 latest colored pictures 2x2
  3. Certification from the Principal that the student is graduating from Senior High School.
  4. Entrance Examination Fee of Php 350.00

Application Process

  1. Present your complete set of requirements for evaluation.
  2. Fill out your application for the admission test (online profiling). Print your application form.
  3. Get “Order of Payment”
  4. Pay your entrance exam fee.
  5. Present your official receipt. Log your name on the list of examinees for PNUAT/GCAT/CTP Admission Test. Claim your test Permit.

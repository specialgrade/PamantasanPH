    • Application Process

    1. Get/secure application form from the:
    2. Guidance Office/Student Services Office of the chosen campus.
    3. Admission and Testing Office, PSU Lingayen, Pangasinan.
    4. Download the form here.
    5. Properly accomplish PSU-CAT application form and attach recent two (2) identical 2 X 2 photo with name tag.
    6. Pay testing fee to designated cashier and get official receipt.
    7. Submit accomplished PSU-CAT form with requirements and the official receipt to the Guidance Office/Student Services Office of chosen campus.
    8. Get examination test permit.
    9. During the Examination day
    10. Go to the designated testing center specified in the examination permit.
    11. Bring Mongol pencil #2.
    12. Present examination permit to the examiner.
    13. For those who passed the PSU-CAT get/secure interview schedule from the campus where PSU-CAT was taken. Result of PSU-CAT
    14. Results will be published thru the internet at the PSU website (www.psu.edu.ph)
    15. Examinees will be sent their individual notice (for those who submitted self-addressed envelope).
    16. Note: Application Forms will be released (2) two weeks before the examination day.

Admission Requirements

High School Graduates

  • Accomplished application form.
  • Form 138 (Report Card).
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character.
  • Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO).
  • 2x2 ID photo.
  • USePAT (USeP Admission Test) results.

Transferees

  • Transfer Credential/Honorable Dismissal.
  • Transcript of Records (TOR) for evaluation.
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character.
  • Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO).
  • 2x2 ID photo.
  • USePAT results, if applicable.

Graduate School Applicants

  • Accomplished application form.
  • Official Transcript of Records (TOR).
  • Certificate of Employment (if applicable).
  • Recommendation letters from former professors/employers.
  • Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO).
  • 2x2 ID photo.

Application Process

  1. Obtain the Application Form: Download from the USeP website or get it from the admissions office.
  2. Fill Out the Form: Complete the form with accurate information.
  3. Submit Requirements: Gather all necessary documents and submit them along with the completed application form to the admissions office.
  4. Take the USePAT: Schedule and take the University of Southeastern Philippines Admission Test if required for your course.
  5. Wait for Results: Admission results will be posted on the USeP website or communicated via the contact information you provided.
  6. Enrollment: If accepted, follow the enrollment instructions provided by the university.

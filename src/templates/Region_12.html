Admission Requirements

  • HIGH SCHOOL GRADUATES:
    • Accomplished application form.
    • Form 138 (Report Card).
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character.
    • Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO).
    • 2x2 ID photo.
    • USePAT (USeP Admission Test) results.
  • TRANSFEREES:
    • Transfer Credential/Honorable Dismissal.
    • Transcript of Records (TOR) for evaluation.
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character.
    • Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO).
    • 2x2 ID photo.
    • USePAT results, if applicable.
  • GRADUATE SCHOOL APPLICANTS:
    • Accomplished application form.
    • Official Transcript of Records (TOR).
    • Certificate of Employment (if applicable).
    • Recommendation letters from former professors/employers.
    • Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO).
    • 2x2 ID photo.

Application Process

  • Step 1: Prepare necessary documents such as the accomplished application form, high school report card (Form 138), certificate of good moral character, birth certificate, ID photos, and USePAT results.
  • Step 2: Submit the documents to the USeP Admissions Office or apply online through the official USeP website.
  • Step 3: Wait for the confirmation of your application and the schedule for the admission test (USePAT).
  • Step 4: Take the admission test on the scheduled date and wait for the results.
  • Step 5: Once accepted, proceed with the enrollment process and submit any additional requirements.

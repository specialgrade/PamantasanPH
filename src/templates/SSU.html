Exam Requirements

  1. Application Confirmation - If applied ONLINE, please PRINT OUT your accomplished application permits sent to your email. If applied ONSITE/WALK-IN APPLICANTS, make sure to bring 1 pc. 1x1 formal ID picture with white background. (You will only be accommodated if there are vacant slots)
  2. 1 PC. 1x1 ID PICTURE with white background. (write your complete name at the back)
  3. MONGOL #2 PENCIL (strictly NO other pencil allowed.)
  4. ORDINARY BALLPEN
  5. ERASER, GLUE, and SHARPENER
  6. UMBRELLA AND WATER (for your convenience)

FOR THOSE WHO DID NOT RECEIVED THEIR APPLICATION FORMS, YOU MAY ACCESS THEM THRU THIS URL: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/18D1WtxORkvYTo27bz0Vcov6vfG4MjWR4?usp=sharing make sure to search for your names.

Admission Confirmation

To view your Final Admission Confirmation. The printed Admission Application Form will serve as your Admission Examination Permit.

FOLLOW THE STEPS BELOW FOR YOUR GUIDANCE:

How to search for your name:

  1. Locate the file (the file name is the date when you applied)
  2. Open the file (you cannot search your name when the file is not opened)
  3. Search your name (click the three dots on the upper right corner and click Find or Ctrl+F)

How to print:

  1. Download the file (the file with your application)
  2. Print only the page with your name
  3. Print with short bond paper

Note: The printed form serves as your PERMIT upon taking the Admission Exam. However, please note that although blank forms have been provided, it does not imply that you should fill them out with a ballpoint pen (as we have not instructed you to do so) or attempt to "edit" them to input your information. Please adhere strictly to the provided instructions.

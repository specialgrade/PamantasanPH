    • Application Process

    1. Open your browser and go to unp.priisms.online/padmission
    2. Select from the dropdown menu the following:
    3. â¢ School Campus (always select Main Campus)
    4. â¢ Application Type
    5. â¢ Classification (select College for 4-year program; or Diploma, Certificate or Associ for 2-year program)
    6. â¢ Grade/Level
    7. Select first, second, and third choice of Academic Program, then, click next.
    8. Fill out all required fields in Personal Information. Then, click next.
    9. Fill out your Educational Background by clicking the 'Create New' button.
    10. Fill out your Current/Last School Attended Information, then, click 'Add.' Then, click next.
    11. Add your photo by clicking the 'Browse Image' button.
    12. Fill out all required fields in Family Information.
    13. Fill out Contact Person in Case of Emergency.
    14. Select one from Father, Mother, or Guardian in the drop-down menu. Then, click next.
    15. Fill out all required fields in Other Information. Then, click next
    16. Upload required documents by clicking the 'Choose File' button.
    17. File Name of your documents should be your LAST NAME, FIRST NAME MI (e.g. DELA CRUZ, CARL F.).
    18. Make sure that your documents are CORRECT and COMPLETE.
    19. Review your Information
    20. (Reminder: Double check that you have placed your correct and active email address).
    21. Click the boxes to confirm that you have read, understood, and accepted the UNP Admission Policies and Data Privacy Policy.
    22. Read carefully the Admission Policy, then, click 'I AGREE' button.
    23. Read carefully the Data Privacy Policy, then, click 'I AGREE' button.
    24. Click Proceed and Submit
    25. (Reminder: Double check that you have placed your correct and active email address before submitting).
    26. Application successfully submitted.
    27. Login to your email account and check notifications sent to you to view the status of your application.
    28. After your application is checked and verified by the Admission Personnel, your TEST PERMIT and HEALTH DECLARATION FORM will be sent to your email address.
    29. Take note of your schedule and venue of exam. Print your test permit and health declaration form to be presented on the date of your examination.

Application Process

USTP CDO Admission and Testing Procedure

College Level

  1. CREATE A NEW E-MAIL ADDRESS FOLLOWING THIS FORMAT LASTNAME.FIRSTNAME@GMAIL.COM
    • For those who have multiple first names, use only either one or two of it (e.g. Rizal.JuanMiguel@gmail.com).
    • Take note of your new e-mail address, its username, and password as this will be used as our primary means of communication to you for instructions and updates.
  2. PREPARE THE FOLLOWING DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS
    • Scanned copy of all pages of Form 138 (Report card) with current grades in Grade 12 saved in PDF format (for College Freshmen)
    • Scanned copy of all pages of Certificate of Grades/Transcript of Records (TOR) (for Transferee and 2nd Courser)
    • Scanned copy or photo of previous School ID (front and back part) or Certificate of Registration or Enrollment (for College Freshmen, Transferee, 2nd Courser)
    • 2x2 photo in white background
    • Scanned copy of all pages of PSA Birth Certificate
    • Scanned copy of Parent's Income Tax Return (ITR) or Affidavit of No Income
  3. COMPLETELY FILL-OUT THE ONLINE APPLICATION FORM AT PRISMS.USTP.EDU.PH/PADMISSION
    • Choose examination type: onsite (for residents near the school campus) or homebased (for residents who are far from the school campus or have travel restrictions)
    • Choose the desired academic year and term
    • Choose the desired school campus
    • Choose the appropriate application type
      • Freshman - a student applicant who is incoming 1st year level of either college or graduate level academic program
      • Transferee - a student applicant who wishes to transfer from other schools and enroll in USTP
      • Cross-Enrollee - a student applicant from another university who wishes to cross-enroll in USTP
      • Returnee - a student applicant who withdrew from the program or was granted a Leave of Absences (LOA) or who went on Absence Without Leave (AWOL) for one semester or more and wishes to re-enroll in the university.
      • 2nd Courser - a student applicant who is a 4-year degree holder and who wishes to enroll in a 4-year academic program in USTP
      • Earning Units - a student applicant who wishes to enroll in Certificate of Teaching (COT).
    • Choose the appropriate department: College
    • Attach all documentary requirements
  4. SUBMITTED DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS FOR INCOMING FRESHMEN WILL BE EVALUATED BY THE ADMISSION OFFICER WHILE TOR FOR TRANSFEREE AND SECOND COURSERS WILL BE EVALUATED BY THE ACADEMIC PROGRAM CHAIR.
  5. IF INCOMPLETE AND INACCURATE DOCUMENTS ARE SUBMITTED, E-MAIL OF FOLLOW-UP WILL BE SENT WHILE E-MAIL OF ACCEPTED APPLICATION WITH EXAM PERMIT, TESTING SCHEDULE AND TEST INSTRUCTIONS WILL BE SENT TO YOUR NEW E-MAIL ADDRESS IF APPLICATION IS DEEMED IN ORDER.
  6. ACCEPTED ONSITE TEST TAKER
    • Get Evaluation Slip from the Admission and Scholarship Office (Ground Flr, Room 1, ITB Building)
    • Pay testing fee of Php 450 (for 2nd Courser) at the Cashier
    • Present your official receipt of payment to the Admission and Scholarship Office to claim your Permit to Take the Entrance Examination with signature and fill out our logbook
  7. ACCEPTED HOMEBASED TEST TAKER
    • Download Exam Permit embedded in the e-mail of accepted application
    • For 2nd Courser student applicants, payment of testing fee of Php 450 will be charged upon further notice
  8. TAKE THE ADMISSION TEST AS SCHEDULED
  9. WAIT UNTIL TEST RESULTS ARE RELEASED

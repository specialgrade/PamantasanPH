ZAMBOANGA STATE COLLEGE OF MARINE SCIENCES AND TECHNOLOGY

Fort Pilar, Zamboanga City, Philippines

Admission Ongoing

Requirements

  1. Accomplished and Printed On-line Application form for college.
  2. Two (2) Pieces Recent 2×2 ID Pictures with Applicants' Name Tag (Surname, First Name, Middle Initial)
  3. Photocopy of Grade 12 Report Card (For Senior High School Graduates)
  4. Photocopy of Transcript of Records (For Transferee/Second Coursers)
  5. Photocopy of NSO/PSA Birth Certificate.

Reminder: Please bring original documents for verification. All submitted documents in compliance with the College Admission Test (CAT) requirements shall become property of ZSCMST and are not to be returned to the applicant.

Application Process

  1. Freshmen-applicant must apply online for the College Admission Test (CAT)
  2. Freshmen-applicant must report to the Testing, Admission and Scholarship Center (TASC) to submit accomplished downloaded application form with complete admission requirements.
    • Senior High School Card
    • Residency Verification Form (From Accounting Office)
  3. Pay 250 pesos admission fee and secure the examination schedule and permit from the College Admission Office.
  4. Freshmen-applicant must take the CAT based on the given schedule of examination.
  5. Secure the Notice of Admission based on the schedule given by College Admission Office
  6. Qualified applicant should be tagged on the academic program of his/her choice based on the academic grade required by the college/center.
  7. Issuance of College Admission Notice and password for online Enrollment.
  8. Proceed for the online enrolment of the academic program of his/her choice.

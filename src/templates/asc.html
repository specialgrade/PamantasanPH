Admission Requirements

  1. Report Card (Form 138)
  2. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  3. Two (2) copies of 2x2 ID Picture with white background and name tag
  4. Photocopy Philippine Statistic Authority (PSA) Birth Certificate
  5. Certificate of Indigency (if applicable)
  6. Barangay Clearance
  7. Police Clearance; and
  8. Long Brown Envelope

Application Process

  1. Online

    2. Access the google forms available in the College

    • Website: asc.edu.ph
    • College Official Facebook Page: www.facebook.com/asceduofficial

    Downloadable Forms

    • You may download the pre-registration form at the College's Website (www.asc.edu.ph)
    • Scan the accomplished form and email it to the Department where you intend to enroll (Refer to the contacts and email attachments in PDF):
    • Copy of Form 138(For incoming First Years)
    • Copy of Philippine Statistics Authority
    • Honorable dismissal (For transferees)
    • Authenticated Birth Certificate (For incoming First Years)
    • Indicate your:
      • Age
      • Permanent Home Address
      • School last attended
      • Course you intend to enroll
  2. Mobile SMS
    • You can call or text the numbers indicated below.
    • Please indicate the data needed:
      • Name
      • Age
      • Birthday
      • Birthplace
      • Father's Name & Mother's Name
      • Permanent Home Address
      • School last attended
      • General Weighted Average (per Form 138) (For incoming First Years)
      • Course you intend to enroll
  3. Walk-in

    4. By appointment

    • Get an appointment by calling or texting the numbers indicated below
    • Physical presence in the campus COMPLYING ALL PROTOCOLS AND RULES OF INTER-AGENCY TASK FORCE FOR INFECTIOUS DISEASE and the College.

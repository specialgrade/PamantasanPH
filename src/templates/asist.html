Admission Requirements

GENERAL REQUIREMENTS
  1. Form 138 or High School report card (For High School Graduates only)
  2. Birth Certificate/Certificate of Live Birth from NSO
  3. Honorable Dismissal (for transferees)
  4. Transcript of Records (for transferees)
  5. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  6. Two copies of ID picture (1x1); two copies 2x2 ID picture
  7. Certificate of Class Rank (for recipients of ASIST Scholarship Award only)
FOR SPECIAL/PART-TIME STUDENTS
  1. Transcript of Records
  2. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  3. Two copies of ID picture (1x1); two copies 2x2 ID picture
FOR OLD-RETURNEES
  1. Seek re-admission certificate from the Admission Office before they are allowed to re-enroll.
FOR CROSS ENROLEES
  1. A student registered in any institution may be admitted to the college provided he/she presents a written permit from the Dean or Registrar of the school last attended setting forth the courses and the total number of units in which the student will be registered.

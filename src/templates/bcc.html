Application Process

  1. Visit the Website
    Visit the site, and you will see the menu icon, and find the Enroll button.
  2. Create account | Account Registration
    In registration of account, you need to fill-out all the fields. After registration, you will recieve an email notification to verify your account. After verification, you will be able to log in.
  3. Submit Credentials
    After logging in, you will be redirected to this section, you need to submit given credential in order for you to be eligible to enroll

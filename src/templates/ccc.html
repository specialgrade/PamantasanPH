    Application Process

    Regular Students

    1. Click this link to register : https://tinyurl.com/nxp3vbdr
    2. A link to the the actual enrollment form will be sent to your email after registering.
    3. Print the enrollment form. Fill out all required information and make sure to provide true and correct data.
    4. Originally sign your enrollment form. Electronic signature is not allowed.

    Old Students

    1. Download the Enrollment form from this link: https://tinyurl.com/nchcb9ke
    2. Print the enrollment form and fill out all needed information. Likewise, make sure to provide true and correct data.
    3. Submit accomplished enrollment form to the Registrar's Office and secure a copy of your grades from previous semester
    4. Proceed with the usual enrollment process

Application Process

  • 2024-2025 - FIRST SEMESTER PRE-REGISTRATION FOR SCHOOLS WITHIN DAVAO ORIENTAL ONLY
    • STEP 1 - Applicants must fill-out the Applicant's Profile Google Forms BEFORE their examination schedules. This google form is NOT A SLOT RESERVATION. If you wish to secure a slot, submit your requirements to your school's designated guidance advocates.
    • STEP 2 - Applicants will prepare their examination requirements and submit them (except payment) to their school's designated guidance advocates. Make sure to comply the complete requirements.
    • STEP 3 - School advocates will drop-off the requirements BY SCHOOL (except payment) at their designated drop-off areas BEFORE the pick-up schedule.
    • STEP 4 - OSCD staff will pick-up the submitted requirements from the drop-off areas for checking. Only those with complete requirements will be included in the final schedule. WAIT FOR YOUR FINAL SCHEDULE TO BE POSTED AND DISSEMINATED.
  • APTITUDE AND SCHOLARSHIP TEST (SUAST) PROCESS FOR A.Y. 2024-2025 - FIRST SEMESTER EXAMINATION DAY PROCEDURES
    • STEP 1 - Applicants will proceed outside their designated testing rooms for attendance and payment of testing fee (150.00). The testing room in-charge will also verify if they have fill-out the Applicants Profile Google Forms.
    • STEP 2 - Applicants will take the entrance examination for 1 hour.
    • STEP 3 - After the final instructions, applicants will leave the testing room.

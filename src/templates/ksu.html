Admission Requirements

  1. Form 138 or its equivalent
  2. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  3. NSO/PSA Authenticated Birth Certificate (Original)
  4. NSO/PSA Authenticated Marriage Certificate (for married Women)
  5. Permit to study in cases of employed students in the government or in private agencies
  6. Must pass the qualifying examination (if any)
  7. Latest 2x2 colored ID Picture with white background

FOR TRANSFEREES

  1. Certificate of Transfer Credential (Honorable Dismissal)
  2. True Copy of Grades
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  4. NSO/PSA Authenticated Birth Certificate (Original)
  5. NSO/PSA Authenticated Marriage Certificate (for married female students)
  6. Permit to study in cases of employed students in the government or in private agencies
  7. Must pass the qualifying examination (if any)
  8. Latest 2x2 colored ID Picture with white background

FOR CROSS ENROLEES

  1. Permit to cross-enroll
  2. Certificate of Good Moral Character

FOR TRANSNATIONAL STUDENTS

  1. Original Transcript of Records in English
  2. Accomplish Personal Data Sheet
  3. Alien Certificate of Registration
  4. Student Visa
  5. Original Passport
  6. Affidavit of Financial Report
  7. Latest 2x2 colored ID Picture with white background
  8. Must meet all the requirements prescribed by the Department of Foreign Affairs and the Bureau of Immigration
  9. Non-refundable application fee of one hundred US dollars (US$100.00)

FOR GRADUATE STUDENTS

  1. Certificate of Transfer Credential (Honorable Dismissal)
  2. True Copy of Grades
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  4. NSO/PSA Authenticated Birth Certificate (Original)
  5. NSO/PSA Authenticated Marriage Certificate (for married female students)
  6. Latest 2x2 colored ID Picture with white background
  7. Permit to study in cases of employed students in the government or in private agencies
  8. Application letter address to the Dean with two (2) Letters of References from two recognized authorities in the discipline or superior/peer for MPA
  9. Non-refundable application fee of one hundred pesos (100.00), subject to change as needed and approved by BOR

FOR LAW STUDENTS

  1. Certificate of Transfer Credential (Honorable Dismissal)
  2. True Copy of Grades
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character from the Dean of School previously attended
  4. NSO/PSA Authenticated Birth Certificate (Original)
  5. NSO/PSA Authenticated Marriage Certificate (for married Women)
  6. Latest 2x2 colored ID Picture with white background
  7. Permit to study in cases of employed students in the government or in private agencies
  8. Police Clearance
  9. NARCOM Clearance
  10. Certificate of Eligibility to the Law Course

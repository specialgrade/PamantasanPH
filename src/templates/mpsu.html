Admission Requirements

FOR SHS GRADUATES

  1. Career Guided Test Result
  2. Form 138 or High School Card
  3. ALS Report Card (for ALS passers)
  4. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  5. Medical Certificate
  6. PSA Birth Certificate (photocopy)
  7. Marriage Certificate (for married female students)
  8. Permit to study from the agency in cases of employed students (government or private)

FOR TRANSFEREES

  1. Career Guided Test Result
  2. Transfer Credential
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  4. Medical Certificate
  5. Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  6. Marriage Certificate (for married female student)
  7. Permit to study from the agency in cases of employed students (government or private)

FOR GRADUATE PROGRAMS

Doctoral Program
  1. Transcript of Records and Transfer Credentials
  2. An addtional of 15 units from the major subjects of MAED program is required to non-MAED applicant seeking admission to the Ed.D. program.
  3. Other Requirements from the schools with which MPSPC has consortium with.
Master Program
  1. An applicant seeking admission to the MBA program and whose undergraduate course is not aligned to the degree sought should have earned 2 years relevant business experience to be determined by the advisory committee or shall be required to take 12 units of subjects from the following clusters: Principles of Management, Financial Management, Principles of Marketing, and Basic Finance.
  2. Applicant seeking admission to the MAED program should have completed 2 years teaching experience. In addition to the two years teaching experience required, non-education graduate applicant is required to take 12 units of professional education subjects form the following clusters: Principles and Methods of Teaching, Assessment of Student Learning, Foundations of Education, and Curriculum Development.
  3. An applicant seeking admission to the MASE and MTE programs must be a graduate of related or allied courses.
  4. Permit to study from the agency in cases of employed students (Government or Private)

Application Process

  1. Online
    • Student enrols online using Smart Phones or Personal Computers with internet connection.
    • Visit www.mpspc.edu.ph/studentportal (for Bontoc Campus)
    • Click Student Registration Information System
    • Fill-up required information
    • Print unofficial copy of assessment OR just copy the reference number of the assessment (Note: Reservation of subjects is valid for 24 hours only)
    • Proceed to the personnel in-charge of registration for submission of requirements, validation and printing of official assessment
    • Proceed to the table of SSDO for processing and validation of school Identification Card
    • Proceed to the table of Scholarship and Grants Office/ Representative for processing of scholarship and grants
  2. Walk-in
    • Student enrols online using computers in the assigned area
    • Visit www.mpspc-studentportal.edu.ph (for Tadian Campus)
    • Visit www.mpspc.edu.ph/studentportal (for Bontoc Campus)
    • Click Student Registration Information System
    • Fill-up required information
    • Print unofficial copy of assessment OR just copy the reference number of the assessment (Note: Reservation of subjects is valid for 24 hours only)
    • Proceed to the personnel in-charge of registration for submission of requirements, validation and printing of official assessment
    • Proceed to the table of SSDO for processing and validation of school Identification Card
    • Proceed to the table of Scholarship and Grants Office/ Representative for processing of scholarship and grants
    • REMINDERS:
      • For Person With Disabiity (PWD) and Pregnant Women, please proceed to the Area of the Guidance Office to process your enrolment.
      • For Foreign Students, please proceed to the Alumni Relations and Job Placement Office to process your enrolment.
  3. Old Students
    • Pay SSC & TALA Fees
    • Secure Pro-Form from your Department
    • Accomplish and have it approved by the Department Chairperson
    • Proceed to NSTP Office (for those enrolling NSTP subject)

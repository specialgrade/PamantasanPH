Admission Requirements

ALS Certificate

  1. One (1) recent 2x2 ID picture with name tag and white background
  2. Good Moral certificate with dry seal
  3. PSA Birth Certificate

Transferees

  1. Transcript of Records (TOR) / Transfer Credential
  2. One (1) recent 2x2 ID picture with name tag and white background
  3. Honorable Dismissal
  4. Good Moral certificate with dry seal
  5. PSA Birth Certificate

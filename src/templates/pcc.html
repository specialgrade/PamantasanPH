Admission Requirements

  1. FOR PCCAT EXAMINEE INITIAL REQUIREMENTS

    • Duly accomplished PCCAT Application Form (w/ 2x2 picture)
    • Form 138 of Grade 11 and 12 (Photocopy)
    • Voter’s certificate from COMELEC (must be a registered resident and voter of Paranaque City)

  2. FOR ENROLLMENT

    • Duly accomplished admission form
    • PSA birth certificate/ marriage certificate if married (original)
    • Voter’s certificate from COMELEC (must be a registered resident and voter of Paranaque City, photocopy)
    • certificate
    • Diploma
    • Form 138 (original)
    • 3 pcs. of 2x2 picture (with white background and printed name)
    • 2 pcs. of long brown and plastic envelope

  3. FOR PASSERS OF ALS/PEPT

    • 2pcs. of long brown and plastic envelope
    • Unique learner identifier (ULI number)
    • To get your ULI number visit: https://t2mis.tesda.gov.ph/Barangay

Application Process

Before registering online, please make sure that applicant has a scanned, clear and readable copy of the requirements mentioned above. (Document/s must be in PDF file or JPEG format)

  1. Access PCC Online Admission Form using this link: https://forms.gle/o2w2oVquGKNFsqxA9
    • (PCC Online Admission form will be open from Monday to Friday, 8:00am – 5:00am)
  2. Fill-out the application and upload the required document/s.
  3. Once the application form has been successfully submitted, to view your response check your registered email (for editing purposes).
  4. PCC Admission Staff shall process and evaluate the submitted requirements.
  5. All qualified applicants will be scheduled for PCC entrance examination. Schedule of examinations will be posted on Paranaque City College FB Page.

Important Note:

  • NO multiple responses will be accommodated.
  • Only applicants who have submitted initial requirements will be processed.

Admission Requirements

  1. FOR ALL APPLICANTS

    • Grade 12 report card with GWA or its Equivalent (SF-9/Form 138)- Photocopy
    • Good Moral Certificate (Photocopy)
    • PSA Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
    • Medical Certificate (Photocopy/Secure after Qualifying)
    • Two (2) ID Pictures (2x2)
    • One (1) Long Brown Envelope

  2. FOR TRANSFEREES

    • TOR and Transfer Credentials

  3. FOR PASSERS OF ALS

    • Certificate of Eligibility for College Admission

Application Process

  1. Fill up the application form downloadable through the PCCM webpage or secure the application from the guidance office;
  2. Submit all the necessary documents to the assigned Guidance and/or Admission Officer.
  3. Secure the admission test at the assigned schedule.
  4. Submit to an interview to be administered by the Program Head or the College of Dean.
  5. See the list of college qualifiers that will be posted on the College bulletin Boards at the College or on the official PCCM webpage.
  6. For the admitted students, sign the Behavior/Contract waiver required and secure enrollment permit/follow enrollment procedures.

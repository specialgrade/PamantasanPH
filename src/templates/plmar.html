Admission Requirements

Qualifications

For Undergraduate Students Enrolling for Graduate Programs:

  1. BONAFIDE RESIDENT OF MARIKINA
  2. Resident of Marikina City for at least three (3) years; and
  3. A graduate of any public or private school located in Marikina.

DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS

For Grade 12 Students Enrolling for Undergraduate Degree Programs:

For Transferees

  1. Resident of Marikina City for at least three (3) years; and
  2. Student who enrolled in other higher educational institutions prior to his/her enrolment in PLMar.

Application Process

FOR FRESHMEN AND TRANSFER APPLICANTS

(Second-year or Third-year students only) should follow these steps:

  1. Sign up for the PLMar Admission Account. A valid email will be required for you to accomplish this step.
    • Verify Email Address. Enter an unregistered email address, then click 'Verify my Email Address' button. Take note that if you have previously created an account with us, you can no longer use that email address. If the entered email address does not have an existing record, proceed to register.
    • Register. Promptly register email address and provide the required information on the form. You will be given your Admission ID after.
  2. Fill-out Application. Enter the Admission login account given to you upon the Sign Up step. Enter the APPLICANT LOGIN area. Once logged in, accomplish the required information.
    • Complete Admission Form. There are 4 sections that make up your Admission Form (Form 1A). Fill-out all sections to proceed to uploading pictures.
    • Upload a recent 2x2 photo. Pictures should have a nametag and must be taken on white background, with neutral facial expression, must not be wearing eyeglasses or colored contact lenses, and with ears exposed if religious customs do not prohibit. Should be in JPEG format.
    • Applicant Declaration. See more information inside the application.
    • Submit Application. Click the SUBMIT ADMISSION APPLICATION button. Once submitted, your application will be sent to the Admission Office.
  3. Submit Required Documents.
    • Print your Admission Form (Form 1A). Use long bond paper (8.5"x13") for printing.
    • Print your Appointment Form Use long bond paper (8.5"x13") for printing.
    • Submit to the PLMar Admission Office. Submit in a Long Brown Envelope and Plastic Envelope the printed Admission Form together with all the requirements listed below at PLMar SSS Campus during the Application Period.
  4. Verification of Application and Requirement. Your application and submitted requirements will be verified by the Admission Office. Application Receipt shall be released if your documents and information are complete.
  5. PLMar College Admission Test. More information regarding the PCAT 2024 will be posted later on.

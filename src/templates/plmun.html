Application Process

Admission Requirements and Procedures for Freshmen of Public and Private High Schools of Muntinlupa

  1. Submit to OUR (Office of the Registrar the following):
    • Form 137/138 (at least with second grading period)
    • Barangay Clearance
    • Parent's Voter's I.D.
    • Birth Certificate (NSO/LCR)
    • 2x2 Pictures 2 pieces (with white name and background)
  2. Fill out the admission/examination permit form.
  3. Pay Php200 to the Accounting Office then submit the OR back to the Guidance Division.
  4. Get examination schedule.
  5. On testing date, please present test permit and receipt./li>
  6. Wait for the posting of exam result posted in PLMun website (www.plmun.edu.ph).
  7. Passers must secure and fill-out the Cumulative Record Form (CRF) from the Guidance Division.
  8. Get the schedule of college interviews from the Guidance Division.
  9. After the college interview, the accepted applicants must return the CRF to the Guidance Division and get the enrollment requirements and schedule.

Admission Requirements and Procedures for Non-Muntinlupa Residents

  1. Submit to OUR (Office of the Registrar the following):
    • Form 137/138 (at least with second grading period)
    • Barangay Clearance
    • Parent's Voter's I.D.
    • Birth Certificate (NSO/LCR)
    • 2x2 Pictures 2 pieces (with white name and background)
  2. Fill out the admission/examination permit form.
  3. Pay Php200 to the Accounting Office then submit the OR back to the Guidance Division.
  4. Get examination schedule.
  5. Present test permit and receipt on the testing date.
  6. Wait for the Posting of Exam Result posted in PLMun website (www.plmun.edu.ph).
  7. Passers must secure and fill-out the Cumulative Record Form (CRF) from the Guidance Division.
  8. Get the schedule of college interviews from the Guidance Division.
  9. After the college interview, the accepted applicants must return the CRF to the Guidance Division and get the enrollment requirements and schedule.

Admission Procedures for Returnee Students

  1. Secure clearance form from OUR.
  2. Report to your college for advising and encoding of subjects.
  3. Pay to the cashier.
  4. Bring OR to OUR for COM (Certificate of Matriculation).
  5. Photocopy of NSO Marriage Contract (if recently married).

Admission Procedures for Foreign Students

  1. Foreign students shall be admitted to PLMun subject to existing regulations of the Commission on HigherEducation (CHED). Other requirements are:
  2. Student Visa (9-F Visa)
  3. Letter of Acceptance from the College of His Choice (original and photocopy)
  4. Affidavit of Support
  5. Latest Passport
  6. Authenticated Birth Certificate (original and photocopy).

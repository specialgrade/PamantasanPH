Admission Requirements

Qualifications

  1. At least 80% General Weighted Average (GWA)
  2. Graduate of Senior High School or ongoing Grade 12 SHS
  3. Applicant/Parents or Guardian should be residents of Pasig City for at least five years.

DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS

  1. Completely filled-out Application Form
  2. CTC Grade 11 Form 138 (for Graduating Senior HS)
  3. CTC F137 with remarks: For Evaluation Purposes only (for Senior Hs graduates)
  4. Any Government issued ID/School ID of applicant
  5. PSA Birth Certificate
  6. Two pcs. Passport size picture, white background with nameplate
  7. Any government-issued ID of Parents
  8. Proof of Income of Parents (ITR, DSWD Case Study, or DSWD Beneficiary ID, etc,)
  9. Notarized Affidavit of Guardianship (for applicants with guardians only)

Qualifications for Transferees

  1. Must be incoming 2nd-3rd Year ONLY from any CHED accredited college/university (must have taken at least 36 units)
  2. Graduated under the K12 Program
  3. GWA of 2.25 or higher
  4. No Failing, Dropped or Incomplete Grades
  5. No Shifting of Program
  6. Parents/Guardians or Applicants should be residents of Pasig City for at least five years.

Application Process

Admission Requirements and Procedures for Freshmen of Public and Private High Schools of Muntinlupa

  1. Online Application
    • Accomplish the Online Registration Application Form via Google Form
  2. Email Notification
    • An email notification for the submission of documentary requirements will be sent via registered email. (You may visit the PLP Admission Office should you fail to receive an email within 2 weeks from online application)
  3. Submission of Requirements
    • Submit all the necessary documents on the given schedule for validation and assessment.
  4. Entrance Examination
    • Examination schedule will be sent via registered email.
  5. College Assessment
    • College Assessment Schedule will be sent via registered email
  6. Posting of Qualifiers
    • List of successful qualifiers will be posted in PLP campus and PLP Official Facebook Page.
  7. Confirmation Slot
    • Qualified applicants are given 2 weeks from the day of posting to confirm their slot.

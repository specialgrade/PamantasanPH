Application Process

Admission Requirements and Procedures for Freshmen of Public and Private High Schools of Muntinlupa

  1. Visit the link http://tinyurl.com/PLVCAT2024. Download and accomplish the PLV-CAT Application Form 2024 and complete the documentary requirements.
  2. Submit the fully accomplished PLV-CAT Application Form 2024 and Documentary Requirements at the office of the University Registrar (OUR, 2nd Floor, Student Center Building, PLV Maysan Campus.
  3. Secure the Examination Permit.
  4. Take the PLV admission Test
  5. View the result of the PLV-CAT 2024

Admission Requirements

Qualifications

  1. Grade 12 students expected to graduate at the end of the academic year from DepED accredited schools
  2. Grade 12 graduates
  3. Completed middle school or its equivalent (e.g. Philippine Educational Placement Test, Alternative Learning System, or a Non-Formal Equivalency Test )

DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS

  1. For Grade 12 Students

    • Certified true copy of Form 137 or Form 138 (Grade 10 and 11) with no grade below 80% across all subject areas and with a general weighted average (GWA) of at least 85% or its equivalent

  2. For Grade 12 Graduates Who Have Not Taken Courses in College

    • Certified true copy of Form 137 or Form 138 (Grade 12) with no grade below 80% across all subject areas and with a GWA of at least 85% or its equivalent

  3. For Grade 12 Graduates Who Have Taken Courses in College (Transferees)

    • OR or certification of grades and Honorable Dismissal

  4. For Those Who Completed Middle School or Its Equivalent

    • Certification from PEPT, ALS, etc. certified by the DepED

Other Requirements

  1. 2”x2” colored ID picture taken within 6 months, with white background and nametag
  2. Certification from the school principal that the student is graduating from SHS/certification of enrollment/registration form
  3. Extra-curricular and leadership certifications
  4. Application essay
  5. Application Form
  6. Signed Data Privacy Consent Form
  7. Two recommendation letters

Application Process

  1. Fill-out Application Form for Documents
  2. Present Request Form at the Accounting Office and secure order of payment
  3. For first-time applicants, a general clearance is required if documents applied for are any of the following: TOR, Diploma, and Evaluation of Grades in the Graduate Level. Secure General Clearance at the Accounting Office. *If not Applicable proceed to STEP 4
  4. Pay the indicated fee for the request and secure the official receipt of the payment at the Cashier’s Office
  5. Present Request Form with Official receipt at the Registrar’s Office

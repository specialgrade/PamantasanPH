Application Process

  1. Get all grades from the first semester of the previous A.Y. from the Office of Registrar
  2. Proceed to the Office of Admission and get your Enrollment Form
  3. Secure a copy of your Evaluation Form or your Prospectus from your Department Head
  4. Proceed to Cashier for your Payments (Tuition, Clearance and others)
  5. Present your accomplished/sugned Enrollment Form to the Registrar's Office for officially enrolled mark and get your class schedules

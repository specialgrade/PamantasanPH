Bago City College

Rafael M. Salas Drive, Brgy. Balingasag, Bago City, Philippines

CLOSED

Admission Requirements

  1. For Freshmen
    • Form 138 (High School Card)
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character
    • Birth Certificate (NSO SECPA)
    • Other requirements specified by the program such as: Medical Certificate & Police Clearance
  2. For Transferee
    • Certificate of Eligibility to Transfer (CET)
    • Transcript of Records (for evaluation)
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character
    • Other requirements specified by the program

    Note: Students enrolling must pass the College Entrance Examination and other qualifying exams as may be required by the respective Department.

Application Process

  1. Present entrance credentials.
  2. Fill up the enrollment form and have it approved by the dean/dept. coordinator.
  3. Submit the enrolment form for assessment and payment of fees (for Non-Bago residents) and have it approved by the College Registrar.

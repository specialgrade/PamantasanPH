Admission Requirements

CMU COLLEGE ADMISSION TEST INFORMATION DRIVE

Information Drive is conducted every year before the CMUCAT is administered. Members of the CMUCAT Board prepare the schedule and duly approved by the University President. The schedule is posted in the CMU Website. CMU College Admission Test (CMUCAT).

REQUIREMENTS:

  • School ID
  • Certification that the student is graduating from the school Guidance Counselor
  • 2 pcs. 1x1 ID picture
  • Testing Fee of Php 200.00 for 2nd courser only.

SCHEDULES

Schedule for examinations is prepared by the CMUCAT Board and approved by the University President and disseminated during the Information Drive. Application forms for the CMUCAT are given to testing centers during the information drive and can be obtained from CASP Office. Notices of CMUCAT are mailed to the testing centers not later than 2nd week of February.

GENERAL ADMISSION POLICIES

All students who have satisfied the quota requirements of the various degree programs of the different colleges based on their CMU College Admission Test (CMUCAT) rating are eligible for admission to the university. All entering first year, transferees, returnees, entering degree holders and foreign students must satisfy the admission requirements of the university namely:

FOR ENTERING FIRST YEAR

  • CMUCAT Notice of Result (2 copies)
  • Original Form 138 (Senior High School Report Card) Original & One (1) Photocopy
  • Photocopy of Parent's Income Tax Return/Any Proof of Income (for free tuition only)
  • PSA original copy of Birth Certificate (LCR Form 1A is required if PSA copy is not clear)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Recent Chest X-Ray Results
  • Medical and Dental Certificate
  • Hepatitis B Ag Test (For Hospitality Management, Home Economics, Food Technology and Nutrition and Dietetics Hepatitis A is required)
  • Marriage Certificate (if married) (Original & one (1) photocopy)
  • Privacy Statement and Consent (2 copies)
  • Print out of your Confirmation Slip
  • Long brown envelope (2pcs)
  • 2×2 ID picture w/ collar and white background (2 pcs)

FOR TRANSFEREES

  • CMUCAT Notice of Result (2 copies)
  • Honorable Dismissal
  • Transcript of Records (w/ remarks for evaluation)
  • PSA original copy of Birth Certificate (LCR Form 1A is required if PSA copy is not clear, Original & one (1) Photocopy)
  • Photocopy of Parent's Income Tax Return/Any Proof of Income (for free tuition only) (5 Photocopies)
  • 2x2 colored ID picture w/ collar and white background (2 pcs)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Recent Chest X-Ray Results
  • Medical and Dental Certificate
  • Hepatitis B Ag Test (For Hospitality Management, Home Economics, Food Technology and Nutrition and Dietetics Hepatitis A is required)
  • Police Clearance
  • Marriage Certificate (if married) (Original & one (1) photocopy)
  • Privacy Statement and Consent (2 copies)
  • Print out of your Confirmation Slip
  • Long brown envelope (2 pcs.)

Application Process

To register, please visit http://devops.cmu.edu.ph/doorstep/

Please bring the following requirements during examination:

  • Duly filled-out and signed application form (printable after online application via doorstep.cmu.edu.ph)
  • Note: Signed by Student and Guidance Counselor or School Principal with 2 pcs 1x1 ID picture.
  • Vaccination card
  • Ballpen (blue/black ink)
  • Facemask and Alcohol
  • School ID for identification (if available)

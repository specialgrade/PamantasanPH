      • Application Process

      1. After Submitting the documents, the Enrolling Adviser will give you the 3 copies of Tentative Enrollment Form (TEF) and fill-out the necessary information.
      2. Proceed to the Dean's Office and drop your Tentative Enrolment Form in the designated drop box for Dean's signature, posting, and assessment.
      3. Wait for your name to be called for the release of your Certificate of Enrollment (COE). COE of students with complete requirements will be stamped "Registered" while those with pending requirements will be stamped "Conditional Enrollment".

        4. Note: Upon compliance of students with pending requirements, the Registrar will stamp their forms, "Registered"

      4. Proceed to the university gymnasium to apply for a Student ID Card, and have your photo taken.

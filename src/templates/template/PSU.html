Admission Requirements

  1. ParSUCAT Application Form
  2. Student Personal Record
  3. Two pcs 2x2 and one pc 1x1 recent ID Picture
  4. School Certification signed by the school principal/registrar (FOR GRADUATING SHS STUDENTS)
  5. Authenticated photocopy of Grade 12 Report Card/F138 (FOR SHS GRADUATES)
  6. Authenticated photocopy of 4th Year HS Report Card/F138 (FOR OLD CURRICULUM HS GRADUATES)
  7. Photocopy of Certificate of Passing/Rating (ALS PASSERS)
  8. Copy of Grades/Transcript of Records (TRANSFEREES)
  9. Color-coded long-sized folder
  10. Additional Requirements for Special Group Applicants:

    • Persons with Disability ID (Differently-Abled Applicants)
    • Solo Parents ID (for children of Solo Parents)
    • Brgy. Certification (for applicants residing in GIDAS)
    • Certificate of Membership from NCIP (for members of indigenous community)

Application Process

  1. Available slots are for applicants who took the ParSUCAT and received the following remarks: BELOW QUOTA, WAITLISTED, or BELOW CUT-OFF SCORE (board courses).
  2. Successful applicants who wish to withdraw their slot and apply to programs located in PSU Campuses (SagÃ±ay, Salogon, Caramoan, Tinambac, and Lagonoy) are allowed depending on the availability of slots.
  3. Only the applicant must transact at the GAP office.
  4. Applicant can only apply to one (1) program.
  5. Applying for listed programs does not guarantee a slot; admission depends on the number of available slots and the ParSUCAT result.
  6. Slots are non-transferable. For applicants who decide not to enroll, the slot cannot be given to another applicant.
  7. Applications are NOT processed on a first-come, first-served basis.
  8. Application will be on May 28-29 and June 3-4, 2024 at the GAP Office, Goa Campus. The GAP Tinambac, and GAP Caramoan offices will also accept applications for Tinambac and Caramoan applicants only.

Application Process

  1. Submit Documents to Admission Office. Register for College Entrance Examination.
  2. Take & Pass the CEE. Claim result slip.
  3. Picture Taking in Admission Office.
  4. Present result slip to the department of your course of choice.
  5. Pass the interview and submit any additional documents required by the department.
  6. Secure Department Enrolment Form.
  7. Bring Enrolment Form to Cashier Office, Billing Office, and Registrar's Office for Recordkeeping and Validation.
  8. You are officially enrolled.
  9. Await class schedule from your respective department.

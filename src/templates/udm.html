Admission Requirements

MUST BE:

  1. Bona fide resident of Manila
  2. Graduates of 2019 (both SHS and Freshman)
  3. High School graduate on or before June 1, 2015 requires Bridging Programs for General Education
  4. Parent must be a REGISTERED VOTER OR TAXPAYER in MANILA
  5. Must be physically fit
  6. Must pass the UDM Admission Test

REQUIREMENTS:

  1. Fill out digital application forms at reg.udm.edu.ph/admission/college or reg.udm.edu.ph/shs
  2. Send Copies of the following to (For incoming 1st year college):
    • admission-stem@udm.edu.ph
    • admission-abm@udm.edu.ph
    • admission-als@udm.edu.ph
    • admission-ga@udm.edu.ph
    • admission-he@udm.edu.ph
    • admission-humss@udm.edu.ph
    • admission-ict@udm.edu.ph
    • admission-tvl@udm.edu.ph
  3. Birth Certificate (NSO/PSO)
  4. Parents latest ITR or Voter’s I.D. or Voter’s Affidavit
  5. Report Card – with 2nd quarter grades (for the 1st Batch) certified correct by the Guidance and/or
  6. Principal with GWA of 83%
  7. Report Card – with complete grades (for the 2nd-3rd Batch)
  8. Certification of Good Moral Character
  9. 2 pcs. of 1 ½ X 1 ½ colored I.D. picture with name tag (white background)
  10. For PEPT, ALS, NFEA&E –Certificate of Passing and Report Card

Application Process

PROCEDURES IN TAKING THE ADMISSION TEST

  1. Wait for you examination permit on your registered email
  2. Print a copy of your examination permit
  3. Go to Universidad de Manila-Main campus on your designated room and schedule
  4. Don’t forget to bring the following:
    • Ballpen
    • Test Permit
    • School ID/Any valid ID/li>
    • Face mask, face shield, alcohol
    • Pencil (at least 2 pcs.)

Application Process

Freshmen and Transfer Applicants

(Second-year or Third-year students only) should follow these steps:

  1. Register online through https://umak.edu.ph/olca/registration/new (for Freshmen) or https://umak.edu.ph/olca/registration/new/transferee (for Transferees & Second Coursers)

    NOTE: Online Application for NEW FRESHMEN is on February 20, 2024 to March 26, 2024, AND TRANSFEREES AND SECOND COURSERS IS ON APRIL 1-7, 2024

    LINK: College Program Requirements for AY 2024-2025

  2. Answer the captcha and submit the application form.
  3. Open the email address you registered in the application form and check for a verification email. Click the “verify” button.
  4. Another email will be sent to you containing your pre-filled application form, list of requirements, and the date when you will submit your admission requirements.
  5. Bring the COMPLETE and CLEAR copies (original and photocopy) of the requirements at the Center for Admission and Scholarship.
  6. Testing Fee of Php 500 is to be paid at the University of Makati cooperative after evaluation of the admission requirements.

Admission Requirements

  1. Original Copy of Form 138 (Freshmen) or Transfer Credentials (if Transferee)
  2. Certification of Good Moral Character issued by the school principal (original copy)
  3. PSA Birth Certificate (photocopy)
  4. Brgy. Clearance
  5. Certificate of Indigency
  6. Two pcs. 1x1 picture
  7. Two pcs. 2x2 picture
  8. Long Brown Envelope
  9. Long Plastic Envelope
  10. PWD Card/Certification photocopy (if applicable)
  11. "Don't Forget to Bring your Ballpen"
  12. First come first serve.

Application Process

  1. Complete your enrollment to Binalatongan Community College in 4 Easy Steps:
  2. Register online by accomplishing the Online Registration Form for New and Transferees.
  3. Write down the examination code and student number that will be provided to you after accomplishing the registration form successfully.
  4. Proceed to entrance examination page, enter the examination code and take the Online Entrance Examination.
  5. Submit your requirements at Binalatongan Community College, Brgy. Ilang San Carlos City, Pangasinan.
  6. REGISTRATION LINK: https://bccedu.site/registration/
  • Note:
  • Write your name and student number at the back of the long brown envelope. You must secure all your documents inside the long brown envelope and put it inside a long plastic envelope.
  • Submit your requirements as soon as possible. You are only given 1 week (from the time you registered online) to submit your requirements. Your registration will be cancelled automatically if we do not receive your documents within your submission period.
  • We may accept submissions with incomplete requirements only if it lacks Form 137 and/or PSA Birth Certificate.

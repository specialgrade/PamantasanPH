Application Process

  • NEW/BEGINNING FRESHMEN STUDENT:
    • Accepted regardless of age, nationality, religion, and political affiliations.
    • Must have graduated from a recognized secondary school in the K-12 program.
    • Must not have enrolled in any academic college subject/s prior to their enrollment as beginning freshmen otherwise they shall be classified as transferees.
    • Not accepted during the Second Semester or during the Summer Term.
    • Students enrolled in vocational courses or those not leading to a degree program shall be classified as beginning freshmen.
    • Must comply with all the other prescribed admission requirements of the University.
    • Must pledge to abide by and comply with all the rules and regulations of the University.
  • TRANSFEREES:
    • Must submit an official Transcript of Records during enrollment (TOR).
    • Must have no failing grade and must have a GPA of 2.0 or higher.
    • Transferees who seek enrollment in any academic program requiring licensure examination must not have a failing grade in any academic and non-academic subjects.
    • Only Students who have enrolled in any course leading to a degree program shall be admitted as transferees.
    • Admission shall be on probation basis during the first term of stay in the University based on the performance and behavior of the student.
    • Transferees from SUC institutions shall be admitted and treated as regular students in accordance with the existing policies of the University.
    • Qualified transferees may be admitted to fill-up the quota of the course applied for in accordance to the existing policies.
  • FOREIGN STUDENTS:
    • Must submit an official Transcript of Records during enrollment (TOR).
    • Must have no failing grade and must have a GPA of 2.0 or higher.
    • Transferees who seek enrollment in any academic program requiring licensure examination must not have a failing grade in any academic and non-academic subjects.
    • Only Students who have enrolled in any course leading to a degree program shall be admitted as transferees.
    • Admission shall be on probation basis during the first term of stay in the University based on the performance and behavior of the student.
    • Transferees from SUC institutions shall be admitted and treated as regular students in accordance with the existing policies of the University.
    • Qualified transferees may be admitted to fill-up the quota of the course applied for in accordance to the existing policies.

Admission Requirements

GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

  1. Photocopy of Grade 12 Report of Grades (1st Semester)
  2. Original PSA Birth Certificate. If not yet available, download the Commitment Form HERE, fill it out, sign, and submit a scanned copy.
  3. Grade 12 Card
  4. Certificate of Good Moral Character

ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS

  • BS Midwifery, BS Nursing and BS Pharmacy
    1. For Female: Hepatitis B Test Result, Chest X-Ray and Pregnancy Test
    2. For Male: Hepatitis B Test Result, and Chest X-Ray
  • BS Hospitality Management, BS Nutrition and Dietetics, and BS Food Technology
    1. Hepatitis B Test Result
